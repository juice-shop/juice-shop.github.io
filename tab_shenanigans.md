## Lego Tower

This specifically designed LEGO tower allows bringing a 4x Raspberry Pi cluster with PoE & cooling fans to any training and have MultiJuicer instances running literally out of the box! Also needed: PoE switch, WiFi router, and Internet uplink via cable e.g. through additional WISP router. Build your own with our [part list and build manual](https://github.com/OWASP/owasp-swag/tree/master/projects/juice-shop/lego).
